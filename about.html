How to

Choose one of the chart presets from a dropdown list and click 'Add chart' button.

Most charts display additional curve 'NaN', which show the data accuracy. Greater value means lower accuracy or insufficient data (lower is better).

Data source

As data source UNODC Percentage of homicides by firearm, number of homicides by firearm and homicide by firearm rate per 100,000 population document was used.
